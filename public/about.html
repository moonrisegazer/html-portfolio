About Me

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm a curious traveler on the winding roads of languages, photography, and design.

Language Enthusiast

My journey began with a fascination for the Chinese language during my undergraduate studies. Picture a young scholar, immersed in the intricacies of characters and tones, unlocking the secrets of a culture rich in history and tradition. This vibrant pursuit not only honed my linguistic skills but also ignited a passion for cross-cultural communication that continues to inspire me today.

Ancient Iranian Languages

With a thirst for knowledge that could not be quenched, I ventured into the realm of ancient Iranian languages for my master’s degree. Imagine dusty scrolls, faded scripts, and the echoes of civilizations long past. Delving into these languages allowed me to explore the narratives and philosophies of ancient cultures, bridging the past with the present and reminding me of the timeless nature of human expression.

Teaching Journey

For the past eight years, I’ve had the joy of teaching English and Chinese. Each classroom is a vibrant tapestry of diverse minds, where I strive to create an engaging environment that sparks curiosity. Whether helping a student navigate the complexities of English grammar or guiding them through the subtleties of Mandarin, I take pride in fostering a love for language that transcends borders.

A Creative Lens

Beyond the classroom, my creative spirit found a home in industrial photography. Capturing the beauty in machinery and the intricacies of production processes, I learned to see the world through a different lens. Each click of the shutter is a story waiting to be told, and I relish the chance to showcase the artistry in everyday life.

Freelance Design Adventures

In addition, I’ve dabbled in freelance graphic design projects, where imagination meets visual storytelling. Each project is a new adventure, allowing me to blend my love for aesthetics with functionality. Whether it’s crafting a logo or designing promotional materials, I thrive on bringing ideas to life in ways that resonate with others.

Thank you for stopping by to learn a little about me! I look forward to connecting and sharing more of my journey with you.
